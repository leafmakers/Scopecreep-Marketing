AI Delegation
Not generic prompts.
Structured execution.
Scope Creep sends your AI agent full project context — sources, constraints, prior decisions, and expected output format. Every delegation is a real job, not a chat message.
01

Context Packs

Bundle brand rules, references, prior decisions, repo links, and constraints into reusable packs. Attach them to any task or agent job — no back-and-forth required.

02

Agent Jobs

Structured AI-executable tasks with role, prompt, source context, expected output, and approval mode. Not a freeform chat — a real assignment with a real brief.

03

Review Workflow

AI drafts move through draft → pending review → approved → shipped. You stay in control. The agent does the heavy lifting. Nothing ships without your sign-off.

04

Scope Intelligence

Detects task explosion, duplicate work, unclear ownership, missing deliverable links, and excessive revisions. Surfaces cut/defer/re-scope recommendations before it spirals.

Ecosystem
Three layers. One project world.
Album OS organizes. Scope Creep executes. Bad Actors personify. Same project, different lenses.
Album OS

The artifact layer.

Projects, slides, cards, ideas, assets, context, documents, deliverables. The structured operating environment where work is organized and presented.

Scope Creep

The execution layer.

Tasks, delegation, work states, AI orchestration, focus, deadlines, progress, scope detection. Where momentum becomes visible and work moves forward.

Bad Actors

The agent layer.

Characters, personas, AI guides. The identity and personality system that shapes how agents work, communicate, and assist across the ecosystem.
